About

Flinging pennies on a table game.

How to Play:

Goal: Players need to sling pennies across the table in between each other and eventually shoot into the goal on the other side.

The pregame settings are chosen to see what the game will go to. Players then start off by flipping a
coin and calling heads or tails to see who starts. The coins will be placed in a triangle formation
and the starting player will then have to break up the 3 pennies. To sling the pennies, a player needs
to hold onto a penny and pull in the opposite direction of where they want it to go. To be able to get
the pennies to the other side of the table, one penny needs to pass between the other two pennies without
touching either. Shooting pennies needs to alternate so the player doesn't shoot the same penny twice in
a row. The pennies need to stay on the table or else that player's turn is over. Once an error occurs or a
player scores a point in the net at the other end of the table, the possession changes to the other player.
